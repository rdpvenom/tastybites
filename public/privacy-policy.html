Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

TastyBites ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when visiting our Website.

Information We Collect

  • Voluntary Data: Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us.
  • Automatic Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device info, referring URL, pages visited, and time on site — collected via server logs and analytics tools.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, web beacons, and pixel tags to enhance functionality, analyze usage, and serve relevant ads.

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve our Website.
  • To send newsletters and recipe updates you opted into.
  • To analyze traffic and visitor behavior using aggregated data.
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • To detect security threats and comply with legal obligations.

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. Opt out at Google Ads Settings or the NAI Opt-Out Page.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Cookies

  • Essential: Required for core functionality.
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data collection.
  • Advertising: Personalized ad delivery and performance tracking.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences.

Manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit site features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Anonymized, aggregated data may be shared with analytics and ad partners. Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption and industry-standard security measures. No system is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information.

Your Rights

Under applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights (CCPA)

Contact us below to exercise these rights. We respond within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes are posted here with a revised date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
